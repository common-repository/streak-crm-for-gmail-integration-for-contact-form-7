Integrate your contact form 7 with Streak CRM for Gmail, Send your contacts directly to Streak CRM pipelines which can be Sales, Support, Orders and many more.

== Description ==

Integrate your contact form 7 with Streak CRM for Gmail, Send contact information to many pipelines at the same time, ability to register the contact as a person or organization, ease to map all fields of the form.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ph4g5igiNMo

Features:
<ul>
<li>Integrate your Contact form 7 with Streak CRM </li>
<li>Create Contact Person or Organization </li>
<li>Ease of mapping custom fields to the form fields </li>
<li>Create unlimited Pipelines in each form </li>
<li>Super easy to set-up! </li>
<li>Enable/Disable each Pipeline as you want </li>
<li>Update existing contacts or organizations </li>
</ul>
